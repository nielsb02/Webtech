Web Accessibility

The World Wide Web, invented by Tim Berners-Lee , is an information retrieval service of the internet. Users are able to access a wide variety of documents that are published on the Web. Since the Web has many advantages, it is essential that everybody can use it, which is made clear by a quote of tim berners-lee. (see image) The practice of making a website usable by as many people as possible is called Web Accessibility.
