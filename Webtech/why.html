Importance of Web Accessibility

Main Reasons

There are many different reason why Web Accessibility is important. These can be grouped to four main reasons, which are:

  • Ethical reasons
  • Reputational reasons
  • Legal reasons
  • Commercial reasons

Ethical reasons

There are 2 reasons as to why Web Accessibility is important from the ethical perspective:

  1. Social inclusion is a noble cause. This means that involving everyone in new information will make sure that society will progress and that nobody will be left behind.
  2. The Web is built on ideas of free access to anything for anyone. For this reason, no one should be excluded from the Web.

Reputational reasons

There are 2 reasons as to why Web Accessibility is important from the Reputational perspective:

  1. It will improve the brand images of organisations. Having more people on the Web will ultimately lead to a better brand image.
  2. According to research, it will also improve the organisational culture. When organisations embrace the noble cause, employees will feel better, creating a better organisational culture.

Legal reasons

There is one reason as to why Web Accessibility is important from the Legal perspective:

The European Union (EU) adopted WCAG 2.1 as the digital accessibility standard in September 2018. It is to prevent any illegal activities. An example of this is the implementation of laws and policies to prevent discrimination due to poor accessibility support. Currently not every country has implemented laws and policies yet, but this legal pressure will only increase.

Commercial reasons

There are 4 reasons as to why Web Accessibility is important from the Commercial perspective:

  1. First of all, over 1 billion people in the world have a recognised disablity. This is a huge market and it is therefore important that websites are adjusted so that these people are also able to use the website.
  2. The second reason is that many accessibility design principles follow general good practices for usability design.
  3. the third reason is that websites will be made device independant, meaning every device is able to use the website.
  4. Last of all, innovation-driven design will often solve unanticipated problems that might occur in the future.
